Take neat, pretty and easy to understand notes

Some tips on how make your notes better

    

  • Use colours that go well together and look pretty
    • 

  • Practise your calligraphy cursive on a separate pages one or two times before starting so you don't waste pages
    • 

  • When you have a second where you don't need to write clean up edges or add some detail
    • 

  • Get some ideas from safari or Pinterest to get a design you know works!
  • 

  • Try different designs and patterns
    • 

  • keep your writing neat and paced. If you miss something you can ask a friend later but chances are you won't! 
    • 

  • Only write the most important parts and don't get caught up on every word
