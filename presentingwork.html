How to be an excellent presentor

How to present your work well in school



Passion
Be Passionate and Engage Your Audience




Genuinity
The audience will respond if you are eager and genuine.




Consider
Always consider what the audience needs and wants to know when preparing your presentation, not what you can say to them.




Pay attention
You must pay attention to your audience's reaction while you deliver the presentation and adjust as necessary.




Focus
While organizing your presentation, keep things straightforward and focus on your main message. 




Eye conact
Establish eye contact with your audience and smile.




Enthusiasm
Be enthusiastic while, nobody likes someone boring!




Make it simple
Make it simple for your audience to comprehend and respond.




Pictures
Create the presentation with pictures.




Sentences
In any presentations keep the sentences short.
